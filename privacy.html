Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to LinkState. Your privacy is a priority in our system stabilization protocols. This policy explains how your data traces are managed.

2. Information Collection

We collect minimal data to ensure system stability:

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and unique identifiers used by advertising services.
  • Game State: Progress, coin balance, and joker counts stored locally on your device.
  • Connectivity Status: Required for system navigation, reward distribution, and ad serving.

3. Third-Party Services

LinkState integrates the following sub-systems:

  • Unity Ads: Used to provide rewarded energy traces (lives) and system skips. Unity may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA or Android Ad ID) and other data as described in their own privacy policy to serve and personalize advertisements.
  • Google Fonts: Used to render our core interface protocols.

4. Data Retention

Most data is stored locally using system-level shared preferences. Removing the application from your device will permanently purge these traces unless backed up via OS-level cloud services.

5. Children's Privacy

LinkState does not intentionally target or collect traces from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe such data has been logged, please contact us immediately.

6. Contact

For inquiries regarding these protocols, please reach out via our official developer channels.
